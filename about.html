About the creator

Evie

I am a creator at heart! I have always enjoyed making party favors for my friends and family. It is such a favorite pastime of mines, and I have become very passionate about it. I decided to share my passion to not just my family and friends but now to everyone out there! Party favors may be small but can make any occassion special and/or make someone feel special.

Party favors aren’t just for parties, but for any type of event or reason! For instance to gift employees, staff, and customers. Really anyone!

All I do are party favors! So it’s my main focus and therefore all party favors are guaranteed to be prestige! Let me give you time back from sitting countless hours making party favors yourself. I will streamline this process for you, all you need to do is just sit back and relax…
