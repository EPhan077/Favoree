Prestige services

Consultation

I provide a one-to-one consultation no matter how small or big the occasion is. This is an opportunity for both me and you to figure out exactly what you need to get the party favors that will best suit the needs of your occasion. Every party, event or reason is unique, therefore, I will customize the party favors and even the budget accordingly. We can go as simply or extravagant with the party favors!

Creation

All party favors are personally created by me and custom - made based upon the needs we discover from our consultation. This is where all the fun begins! I work on the party favors from start to finish: design concept to selection of products. Every stage of the process, I will communicate the progress to provide you an opportunity to give feedback and make any necessary changes as needed.

Delivery

As mentioned, every party favors are customized. Therefore, the time of completion will vary. I will guarantee that I will meet every deadline discussed. Progress updates will also be communicated should there be any changes to delivery. I will personally hand deliver the completed party favors to you if you are local or provide fast shipping to you. If there are any errors I will fix free at no charge.
